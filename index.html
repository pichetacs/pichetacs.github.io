What is Moodle?

Moodle is a powerful, open-source learning management system (LMS) that makes online learning engaging and effective. It provides educators with a flexible platform to create interactive courses, manage assignments and assessments, and foster collaboration among learners.

Why choose Moodle?

  • **Free and open-source:** No licensing fees, customize and extend as needed.
  • **Flexible and adaptable:** Cater to diverse learning styles and course types.
  • **Feature-rich:** Interactive tools, quizzes, forums, and more.
  • **Community-driven:** Supported by a vibrant community of developers and educators.
